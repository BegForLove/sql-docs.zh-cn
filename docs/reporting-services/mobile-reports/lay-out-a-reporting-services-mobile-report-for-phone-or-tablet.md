# <a name="lay-out-a-reporting-services-mobile-report-for-phone-or-tablet"></a>为手机或平板电脑设计 Reporting Services 移动报表
在 [中创建](../../reporting-services/mobile-reports/create-a-reporting-services-mobile-report.md) Reporting Services 移动报表 [!INCLUDE[SS_MobileReptPub_Short](../../includes/ss-mobilereptpub-long.md)]后，即可设计手机和平板电脑版移动报表。  
  
![SSMRP_SalesTrendRptLayout](../../reporting-services/mobile-reports/media/ssmrp-salestrendrptlayout.png)   
*移动报表母版布局。*  
  
1. 在 [!INCLUDE[SS_MobileReptPub_Short](../../includes/ss-mobilereptpub-short.md)] 的右上角，选择布局图标，然后选择“手机”  或“平板电脑”  。  
  
   ![SSMRP_LayoutMenu](../../reporting-services/mobile-reports/media/ssmrp-layoutmenu.png)  
     
   将打开手机布局网格。 “布局”选项卡上显示了所有可视化效果  。  
     
   ![SSMRP_LayoutGrid](../../reporting-services/mobile-reports/media/ssmrp-layoutgrid.png)  
     
2. 将可视化效果拖动到网格并调整其大小。  
  
   ![SSMRP_PhoneLayout](../../reporting-services/mobile-reports/media/ssmrp-phonelayout.png)  
     
3. 选择“预览”查看报表在手机上的样式  。  
  
   ![SSMRP_PhonePreview](../../reporting-services/mobile-reports/media/ssmrp-phonepreview.png)
